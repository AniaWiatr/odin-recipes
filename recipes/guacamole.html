Guacamole

Description

This is my favorite guacamole recipe that my wife, Emily taught me many years ago.

During the times, when we lived in the UK, it was the very first time when I was introduced to Mexican food.

Emily taught me this recipe, and since then I was hooked!

Ingredients:

  • 3 Ripe Avocados
  • Bunch of fresh cilantro
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Half of small red onion
  • Handful of cherry tomatoes
  • 1 small jalapeno
  • 1 lime or 2 Tbsp lime juice
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Cut avocados half ways, discard the stones and extract the pulp of avocados into a medium bowl. Slightly mash with fork. It is up to you how smooth or chunky you want it.
  2. Finely chop the onion and jalapeno and add to the bowl
  3. Using garlic masher, extract garlic to the mixture
  4. Finley chop cilantro leaves and add to the bowl
  5. Cut cherry tomatoes and add
  6. Add lime juice, salt and pepper and mix all ingredients using a big spoon

Taste and adjust salt or lime juice to your liking. ENJOY!
